Food Compilation Unit #6

Components

  • Solanum tuberosum (mashed variant) - 200g
  • Gallus gallus domesticus ovum (beaten) - 2 units
  • Lactuca sativa (shredded) - 50g
  • Sodium chloride crystals - 1 tsp
  • Capsicum annuum (red, diced) - 30g
  • H2O (liquid form) - variable
  • C12H22O11 (granulated) - optional, for emotional complexity

Preparation Protocol

  1. Combine Solanum tuberosum with H2O. Apply heat until structural integrity is compromised.
  2. Introduce Gallus gallus domesticus ovum to the mixture. Stirl until cohesion is achieved.
  3. Incorporate Lactuca sativa and Capsicum annuum. Mix until distribution is statistically uniform.
  4. Apply Sodium chloride crystals. Taste receptors will acknowledge presence.
  5. Otional: Add C12H22O11 to sumulate sweetness. This may cause emotional responce.
  6. Serve in a containment vessel. Consumption is encouraged but not mandatory.
